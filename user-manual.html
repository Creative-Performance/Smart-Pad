Welcome to the user manual. This guide will help you understand the product and its features.

2.0 Setup Instructions

To prepare the drum pad for use, please follow the following instructions:

  1. Place the drum pad indoors on a stable, flat surface.
  2. Press the power switch to turn on the pad. The LCD should come to life and show the power level of the drum pad in percentage.
  3. Open the app on your phone or mobile device.
    • Press the three dots at the top right corner.
    • Select “Bluetooth”.
  4. On the Bluetooth page, hit the “Scan for Devices” button.
    • Wait until a device comes up.
  5. Hit “Connect” next to the device.
    • Wait until the Pad’s LCD says “Connected”.
  6. Press the start button on the Graph Page.
  7. Once the session is complete, hit the “Stop” button.
  8. A text input will pop up to name the session.
    • Press “Save” to save a session.
    • Press “Cancel” to delete a session.
  9. Saved sessions can be found on the Data Page.

3.0 How to Use

Here’s how you can use the product effectively:

Metronome Page

  1. Input the desired BPM in the BPM text box.
  2. Select the time signature based on the music sheet.
    • The “Bar Length” is the bottom number.
    • The “Beats per Bar” is the top number.
  3. Select the number of “Clicks per Beat”.
  4. Choose the desired sound output.
  5. Press “Start”.
    • The visual and audio displays will start.
    • The visual display will also play on the graph screen.
  6. Option to turn the sound output on and off.
  7. When done, press “Stop”.

Data Page

  1. Saved sessions will automatically be listed in alphabetical order.
  2. You can filter sessions based on title or length.
  3. Press the Replay button to open the graph at the bottom of the page.
    • Enter the BPM that was used for that session to verify the accuracy of hits.
    • The graph can be zoomed in and out for easier viewing.
  4. Press the Download button to download the CSV file to your phone.
  5. Press the Share button to share the CSV file with anyone.
  6. Press the Delete button to permanently delete the session.

Settings Page

  1. Toggle between Light and Dark Mode.
  2. Change the Accent Colour of the App from the dropdown list.
  3. Open the user manual.
