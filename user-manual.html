2.0 Setup Instructions

To prepare the drum pad for use, please follow the following instructions:

  1. Place the drum pad indoors on a stable, flat surface.
  2. Press the power switch to turn on the pad. The LCD should come to life and show the power level of the drum pad in percentage.
  3. Open the app on your phone or mobile device.
    • Press the three dots at the top right corner.
    • Select “Bluetooth”.
  4. On the Bluetooth page, hit the “Scan for Devices” button.
    • Wait until a device comes up.
  5. Hit “Connect” next to the device.
    • Wait until the Pad’s LCD says “Connected”.
  6. Press the start button on the Graph Page.
  7. Once the session is complete, hit the “Stop” button.
  8. A text input will pop up to name the session.
    • Press “Save” to save a session.
    • Press “Cancel” to delete a session.
  9. Saved sessions can be found on the Data Page.
